About ESN Germany

The Erasmus Student Network Deutschland e.V. is the consolidation of the German local Erasmus student initiatives. In 38 sections our voluntary members take care of approximately 10.600 exchange students every year. In Europe there are local ESN sections at more than 500 higher education facilities in more than 40 countries. This makes us one of the largest student initiatives in Europe.
